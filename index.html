Biñan, Laguna

This is Biñan, Laguna. Founded by Captain Juan de Salcedo at the end of June 1571. One of the southern province of Laguna that made with pancakes and patries. Most of the food in Biñan is made with rice cake called Puto Biñan. The city is known as the shoes and slippers made and manufactured by Dela Paz and Malaban, while in Barangay Platero was made with hats called "Sumbrero." Biñan, Laguna is located beside on top in San Pedro, Laguna, In Carmona, Cavite on the west, and south is the Sta. Rosa Laguna. This city is nearer at the Metro Manila area.
